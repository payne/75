75 Ohm Coax for Amateur Radio

F Connectors
Pre-terminated Most store-bought cables come with F connectors already attached. Ready to use.
Compression Gas-tight, weatherproof, very reliable. What professional CATV installers use. — Recommended
Crimp-on Requires a crimp tool. Good connection when done properly. Widely available.
Screw-on (twist-on) No special tools needed — just strip and twist on. Convenient but least reliable long-term.
Push-on Quick-connect, no threading. Handy for temporary setups. Not recommended for permanent installations.
F connectors are rated to several GHz in the CATV industry. They are a proper 75Ω connector — arguably better engineered than the PL-259.
